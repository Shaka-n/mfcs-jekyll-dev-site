Moving Forward on the Path of Life

Are you experiencing a difficult situation in your life, in your relationship or in your family? Services Offered

Conflict and stress, uncertainty and doubt, are part of life. Sometimes it seems that the problems can't be solved and that the questins have no answers. The therapists at Milford-Franklin Counseling Services provide confidential counseling services for adults and children who are faced with personal crisis or emotional problems such as:

  • Depression, Anxiety
  • School Adjustment
  • Parent-child Problems
  • Stress Management
  • Low Self-Esteem
  • Marital Problems
  • Separation and Divorce
  • Single Parent and Step-Family Problems
  • Family Problems
  • Adult Children of Alcoholics
  • Women's Issues
  • Men's Issues
  • LGBTQ Issues
  • Sexual Abuse
  • Geriatric (aging) Concerns
  • Eating Disorders
  • Loss and Grief
  • Bereaved Parents
  • Substance Abuse
